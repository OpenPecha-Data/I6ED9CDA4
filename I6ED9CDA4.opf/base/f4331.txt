§20 athāyuṣmān ānandas trāṇamuktaṃ bodhisattvam evam avocat | kathaṃ kulaputra parikṣīṇāyuḥ punar nivartate |trāṇamukto b
odhisattva āha | na tvayā bhadantānanda tathāgatasyāntikāc chrutam santi navākālamaraṇāni | teṣāṃ pratikṣepeṇa mantrauṣa
dhiprayogā upadiṣṭāḥ |santi sattvā vyādhitā na ca guruko vyādhiḥ kiṃ tu bhaiṣajyopasthāyakavirahitaḥ | yadi vā vaidyā ab
haiṣajyaṃ kurvanti | te tato nidānaṃ kālaṃ kurvanti idaṃ prathamam akālamaraṇam |dvitīyam akālamaraṇaṃ yasya rājadaṇḍena
kālakriyā |tṛtīyam akālamaraṇaṃ ye 'tīva pramādavihāriṇas teṣām amanuṣyā ojo 'paharanti |caturtham akālamaraṇaṃ ye agni
dāhena kālaṃ kurvanti |pañcamam akālamaraṇaṃ ye ca udake mriyanti |ṣaṣṭham akālamaraṇaṃ ye siṃhavyāghravyāḍacaṇḍamṛgamad
hyagatā maranti |saptamam akālamaraṇaṃ ye giritaṭāt prapatanti |aṣṭamam akālamaraṇaṃ ye viṣakākhordavetāḍānuprayogeṇa ma
ranti |navamam akālamaraṇaṃ ye kṣuttarṣopahatā āhārapānam alabhamānāḥ kālaṃ kurvanti |imāni saṃkṣepeṇa navākālamaraṇāni 
tathāgateṇa nirdiṣṭāni | anyāni cāprameyāṇy akālamaraṇāni ||
